About Lundahl Ironworks Company

Lundahl Ironworks Company (LIC) is a privately owned fabricator headquartered in Logan, Utah with fabrication facilities in Franklin, Idaho and Logan, Utah. LIC began in the early 1990’s as a specialty supplier to a local general contractor. By 1997, the demand and success forged LIC to move their operations to a larger facility.

 

Using state of the art equipment and the latest technology enables LIC to provide quality work that ensures every customer’s projects are a success. As an AISC Certified Fabricator, we follow a rigorous quality assurance program that is audited and certified yearly by the AISC.
